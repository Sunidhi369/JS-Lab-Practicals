About Smart India Hackathon

The Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative by the Government of India to provide students with a platform to solve some of the pressing problems faced in our daily lives. It promotes innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills among young minds.

Achievements

Over the years, SIH has empowered thousands of students to innovate and create impactful solutions for industries and government organizations.
